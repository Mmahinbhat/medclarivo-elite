MedClarivo Elite — Ticket Submission Policy

1. Confidentiality. Your name and identity will only be disclosed to MedClarivo's Trust & Safety team. Your mentor will not be informed of who raised the ticket during the investigation period.

2. Response commitment. Our team will contact you within 24 hours of ticket submission to acknowledge your report and outline next steps.

3. Investigation process. All reports are investigated impartially. Both parties may be contacted separately. No action is taken solely on the basis of a single report without investigation.

4. Mentor rights. The mentor subject to investigation will be informed of the general nature of the complaint (not your identity) and given an opportunity to respond.

5. Outcomes. Depending on investigation findings, outcomes may include a formal warning, mentorship reassignment, temporary suspension, or permanent removal of the mentor from the platform.

Warning: False or Malicious Reports

If an investigation determines that a ticket was submitted in bad faith, for entertainment, or with intent to harm a mentor's reputation without factual basis:

  • The student's membership will be permanently terminated without refund.
  • The matter may be referred for legal proceedings for defamation or harassment.
  • A permanent record will be maintained and may affect future enrolment on any MedClarivo platform.

6. Privacy. All ticket data is stored securely and is only accessible to authorised Trust & Safety personnel. It is never shared with third parties except where required by law.

7. Withdrawal. Once submitted, a ticket cannot be withdrawn as it triggers a mandatory review process. You may, however, provide additional information at any time by contacting support.
